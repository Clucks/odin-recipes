Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a dish composed of noodles, which are a type of flour based carbohydrate, and a tomato based sauce.

In order to create spaghetti you must first boil noodles into they are about four fifth of the way cooked. Then after cooking the noodles drain the pasta water but leave some for the tomato sauce. Bring the tomato sauce to a boil and mix the sauce and the noodles together and add pasta water into the desired thickness. Afterwards serve and plate. Enjoy!
